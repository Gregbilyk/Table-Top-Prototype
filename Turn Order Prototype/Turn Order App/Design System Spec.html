Principles
Editorial restraint

Solid backgrounds, no gradients or patterns. Let the type carry it.

Snappy & confident

200ms default motion, ease-out. No bouncing, no slow fades.

Systematic color

Four families, fixed scale steps. Never invent a new hue.

Rules
  • Use "I", never "Greg" or "we."
  • Sentence case for body, titles, nav, buttons.
  • ALL CAPS sparingly — hero & dividers only.
  • Em dashes — with spaces. Oxford commas, yes.
  • Spell out one–nine; numerals from 10 up.
  • No emoji in product surfaces.
